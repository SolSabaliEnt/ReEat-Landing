Common questions

Start here.

I cannot sign in or did not receive an email.
Check the spam or junk folder for the address used with Re:Eat. Confirm that the email address is spelled correctly, then request a new verification or password-reset email. If it still does not arrive, contact support@reeatapp.com.
How do I cancel Household+?
Subscriptions purchased through Apple are managed through your Apple ID. Open iPhone Settings, tap your name, choose Subscriptions, select Re:Eat and follow Apple’s cancellation options. Deleting the Re:Eat app does not automatically cancel an active subscription.
How do I restore a purchase?
Use the Restore Purchases option in the Re:Eat subscription or account area while signed in with the Apple ID that made the purchase. If access does not return, send support the Apple transaction date and the Re:Eat account email. Do not send full payment-card details.
How do I delete my account and data?
Open Re:Eat, go to More or Account, and choose the account-deletion option. Follow the confirmation steps to request deletion of the account and associated household data that Re:Eat is not legally required to retain. If you cannot access the app, email privacy@reeatapp.com. Cancel any Apple subscription separately through Apple’s subscription settings.
What should I include in a bug report?
Include the screen or feature involved, what you expected, what happened, the approximate time, your device model and iOS version, and a screenshot when possible. Never include passwords, verification codes or full payment information.
Where can I read the policies?
Read the Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions. These pages are also suitable for the corresponding App Store links.

Contact Re:Eat

Email support@reeatapp.com. For privacy or account-deletion requests, email privacy@reeatapp.com.
