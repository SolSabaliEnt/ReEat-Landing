Privacy Policy

Your household food data should be handled with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how Re:Eat collects, uses, shares, retains and protects information when you use the Re:Eat app, website and related services.

Effective date: June 24, 2026

On this page 1. Scope 2. Information we collect 3. How we use information 4. Household sharing 5. Service providers and AI 6. Sale and advertising 7. Retention and deletion 8. Security 9. Children and Kid View 10. Your choices and rights 11. International processing 12. Changes 13. Contact
Plain-language summary: Re:Eat uses account and household food information to provide planning, groceries, personalized plates, leftovers, kitchen tasks and household memory. Re:Eat does not sell personal information or use household food data for third-party behavioral advertising.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to Re:Eat’s mobile application, marketing website, support channels and related services. It does not govern third-party services that have their own privacy policies, such as Apple’s App Store or services you choose to open from Re:Eat.

2. Information we collect

Account and profile information

We may collect your name, email address, authentication identifiers, account status, profile image and settings used to create and secure your Re:Eat account.

Household food information

Re:Eat is designed to organize a shared household food system. Information you or other household members add may include:

  • Household member names, nicknames, avatars, roles and invitations.
  • Meal plans, recipes, ingredients, cooking steps, grocery lists, pantry or inventory information, leftovers and meal history.
  • Plate preferences, eating styles, dislikes, allergies, sensitivities, texture preferences, dietary goals and household food notes.
  • Kitchen tasks, assignments, schedules, meal times, reminders, Kitchen Memory and Family Recipe Vault content.
  • Feedback about meals, including what the household enjoyed, changed or rejected.

Some food-preference information may be sensitive. Add only information you are comfortable storing and sharing with members of your household.

Usage, device and diagnostic information

We may collect device type, operating-system and app version, IP address, timestamps, feature interactions, crash information, performance logs and security events. We use this information to operate, protect and improve Re:Eat.

Communications and support

When you contact us, we collect the information in your message and any attachments you choose to provide.

Subscription information

When you purchase Household+ through Apple, Re:Eat may receive subscription status, product, transaction and entitlement information needed to provide access. Apple processes payment-card and billing information; Re:Eat does not receive your full payment-card number.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Create, authenticate and maintain accounts and households.
  • Provide weekly planning, recipes, personalized plates, groceries, leftovers, kitchen tasks, role views and household food memory.
  • Sync household activity and show the right information to the right household member.
  • Send transactional messages, invitations, confirmations, reminders and service notices.
  • Provide customer support and respond to privacy requests.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access and security incidents.
  • Monitor reliability, troubleshoot errors and improve product performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

4. Household sharing

Re:Eat is collaborative. Information added to a household may be visible to other authorized members of that household according to their role and the product surface they use. For example, a shopper may see grocery information, a helper may see assigned tasks, and a parent-controlled Kid View may show a child’s meal or helper task.

The household organizer is responsible for inviting appropriate people, maintaining accurate household membership and obtaining permission before adding another person’s information.

5. Service providers and automated features

We may share information with vendors that process data on our behalf to provide hosting, authentication, database storage, file storage, transactional email, application delivery, diagnostics, subscription validation, security and customer support. Current infrastructure may include Supabase, Render, Resend and Apple services.

Some Re:Eat features may use automated or AI-assisted processing to generate planning, recipe, plate, grocery or leftover suggestions. When you choose such a feature, the information reasonably needed to complete the request may be sent to an AI service provider acting on Re:Eat’s behalf. Do not enter passwords, payment-card details or unrelated sensitive information into suggestion fields.

Service providers may use information only as permitted by their agreements with Re:Eat and applicable law.

6. Sale of personal information and advertising

Re:Eat does not sell personal information. Re:Eat does not use household food information for third-party cross-context behavioral advertising. If these practices change, this Policy and any required consent controls will be updated before the new practice begins.

7. Retention and deletion

We retain information while your account is active and as reasonably needed to provide Re:Eat, maintain security, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary by the type of information and why it is stored.

You can initiate account deletion from Re:Eat’s account settings. Deleting an account is intended to delete or de-identify the account and associated personal data that Re:Eat is not legally required to retain. Shared household content may be retained for remaining household members when appropriate, but it will be disassociated from the deleted account where reasonably possible.

If you cannot access the app, contact privacy@reeatapp.com. Deleting a Re:Eat account does not automatically cancel a subscription managed by Apple.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Keep your account credentials private and notify us if you believe your account or household has been accessed without permission.

9. Children and Kid View

Re:Eat accounts are intended to be created and managed by adults or legally authorized household organizers. Kid View is a parent-controlled household surface and is not intended to let a child independently create an account or administer a household.

Parents and guardians should add only the minimum child-related information needed for the household’s food planning. If you believe a child provided personal information without appropriate authorization, contact us so we can review and delete it where required.

10. Your choices and privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability or restriction of certain personal information, or to object to certain processing. You may also withdraw consent where processing relies on consent.

To submit a request, email privacy@reeatapp.com. We may need to verify your identity and household authority before completing a request. Authorized agents may be required to provide proof of authorization.

You can also change many preferences directly in Re:Eat, leave a household where the feature is available, manage notification permissions through device settings and manage Apple subscriptions through your Apple ID.

11. International processing

Re:Eat and its service providers may process information in countries other than the one where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers and apply the protections described in this Policy.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy as Re:Eat changes. We will post the updated version at this URL and revise the effective date. When a change materially affects your rights or how household data is used, we will provide additional notice when required.
